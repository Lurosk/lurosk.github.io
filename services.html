Our Services



Lawn Mowing

No job is too big or too small. Motorised lawn mowers are used for large jobs so that they are completed quickly. All grass clippings are removed and taken away at the end of the job. Jobs can be completed with 24 hours notice. If the customer is not completely satisfied with the job, we will return and redo the job to the customers satisfaction.

Land Scaping

Our gardeners have detailed landscaping training and can design and complete landscaping requirements that you may have. Please call Murray for a detailed quote.

Tree Trimming

Tree trimming can be carried out on trees under three metres in height. If you require trimming of large tress, call Murray for a detailed quote. We can also arrange for trees and stump removal if required.
